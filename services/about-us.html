Our Services

We all know the importance of planning and the fruit it bears when executed effectively. We at K-Arc Square, help you with getting started on your dream house project where you and our team collab to explore every possibilities you can ever think of. We package entire essentials that your house builder or contractor may ask you at the time when you get started with putting your virtual house into ground reality.

  • We build 2D & 3D floor plans with 360 degree panoramic virtual tour of every house corner.
  • The interior and exterior lively walkthrough videos that'll make you feel like home.
  • Plumbing and electrical fixtures layout drawings for your house.
  • Structural design analysis of your house to understand strength & rigidity to resist all applied loads without failure during its intended life.
  • Project cost estimation & scheduling.

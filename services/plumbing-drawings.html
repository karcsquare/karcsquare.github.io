House plumbing system includes pipeline size and location, fixture positions, storage tanks, valves, hot-water heater locations, drainage and routing to outside sewers. Having floor maps in hand, planning plumbing system layout would become an ideal step towards efficient, choke-free and everlasting water supply & drainage system of your house. A fair demand to maintain decent water pressure and circumvent any piping leakage or puncture, require a well planned plumbing layout design that implements efficient techniques and professional standards.
